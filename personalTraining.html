Personal Training: What is it?

My opinion of a personal trainer is someone that takes the time to sit down and listen to what you want to achieve.

Creating programs and progress charts to ensure you stay on track while making better choices not only for your body but for your over all wellbeing and attitude towards life.

PT sessions will include a range of different training exercises and equipment such as:

  • Balance and coordination training
  • Resistance training
  • Suspension training
  • Boxercise
  • Strength and conditioning
  • Kettlebells
  • Kickboxing




What to expect

Before you take me on as your PT first I do a free consultation about what you want to get out of the sessions, your goals and for me to get a idea of your overall health.

From there you can expect me to go above and beyond for you in terms of creating a folder for you that has different workouts in it as well as keeping a record of your progress.

I will check up on you and answer all your questions that you may have, your sessions will be interesting and challenging but only to help you go beyond what you are capable of.

It’s not just your session your paying for you’ll be paying for things I will do for you in the background to help you towards your goals
